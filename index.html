Intentional Tech

Student Testing Trends and EdTech spending

Two self-contained tools examining student achievement and educational technology spending in the San Mateo–Foster City School District. No install, no server — each page runs entirely in your browser.

Interactive Dashboard (Public Record)

CAASPP 10-Year Trends

An interactive dashboard of the district's Smarter Balanced (CAASPP) results from 2014–15 through 2024–25. Filter every chart at once by subject, school, grade, and student group to see which skills eroded, which bands moved, and how many students were tested each year. All data is embedded, so it works fully offline.

Interactive Dashboard (Public Record)

District Vendor Ledger

A ledger of the district's software and curriculum purchases, styled as a public record. Search, filter by category (Assessment, Curriculum / Software, AI Platform, Printed Materials), and sort by amount, vendor, or term start. Summary stats update with your filters, and you can add your own records — saved locally in the browser.
